Welcome to Popular Fittings: Your Trusted Industrial Fittings Manufacturer

As a leading **manufacturer, stockist, and supplier** of all types of industrial flanges and fittings, Popular Fittings is your trusted partner for high-quality industrial components.


Established in 2004, Popular Fittings in Mumbai has grown to become a premier stockist and manufacturer of CS flanges and a wide range of industrial fittings. Our family-owned business prides itself on delivering high-quality products, ensuring customer satisfaction, and providing superior services to our clients across India. Learn more about our legacy and commitment.


Founded by the experienced Mr. Istiyaque Ahmed, Popular Fittings is dedicated to providing reliable quality solutions for critical applications in the marine industry, petroleum plants, oil solvent plants, water treatment plants, fertilizer plants, and various other heavy mechanical industries.
